    ANNOUNCEMENT, Thu Nov 26 2020
        Rebble is an organization dedicated to bringing the abandoned Pebble watches back to
      life. Rebble Grants are their new plan to reinvest some of the proceeds from running the
      Rebble Web Services into development that keeps Pebble watches – and the Rebble service! –
      running well into the future. They’ve been saving up a little money over the past few years
      (!) of running the service and they’ve allocated $25,000 to fund all kinds of things that
      might make the world a little better for Rebblers! Some of the things they hope to fund are:

    

  • work on a replacement mobile app for Pebble;
    • 

  • development on the RebbleOS firmware replacement project;
    • 

  • design work that supports the Rebble Alliance’s goals;
    • 

  • new features for Rebble Web Services; and
    • 

  • development on new watchapps or watchfaces.
    • 


      Maybe we could work on one of these? More info here.
